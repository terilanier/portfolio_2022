SPI Home & Viridian Bay

I work for a home and garden décor company, based in South San Francisco. SPI Home is a cast-metal sculpture company with a B2B wholesale branch and a B2C online and catalog branch— Viridan Bay. Both require website graphics, social media campaigns, and print promotions. Our team is responsible for Juniper Commerce CMS and Shopify CMS, utilizes MailChimp and EMMA for emailing promotions 3 times/week, and produces a minimum of 8 product catalogs a year for Viridian Bay and 1 annual full-line catalog for SPI. We manage online product categories and coordinate with the product developer to establish photo styling for upcoming print and web use.
