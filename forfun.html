Gala Program

A choir director in my community was honored with a gala and fundraising event for his retirement. In designing the graphics for the RSVP card and event program, I utilized a trademark tool of the trade, the tuning fork, with which he always carries.

Geometric Raven

This was a personal illustration challenge to incorporate textures and geometric shapes into an animal drawing. Ravens are particularly recognizable from a profile view; it was my goal to bring its Viking folklore into the design through patterning, as well as to depict it from a front, symmetrical view.

Conceptual Design

I assisted a landscape artist in a mock-up of proposed gate. This was used to show a client how pre-fabricated panels could be used for a rolling privacy wall.


Pattern Design

To complement my intended sewing projects, I will sometimes create custom fabric patterns. This celestial pattern is inspired by origami airplanes and stars.

School Art Item

With the help of a photographer, I arranged students' arms into poses for an art auction project. From six separate photos, I produced this photomontage with careful masking and color manipulation. The final was printed and foam mounted at 36" wide.

Typography

For a final class project, I researched the typeface Sabon. Its use in an historic prayer book led me to craft my final project in the style of the original prayer book, utilizing classic grid structures and a prayer-like vocabulary within.
