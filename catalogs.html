Producing new catalogs with my team is one of my favorite events. This is an opportunity to start afresh with new collections or themes - every few months. Each one is a mad scramble to arrange product themes based on inventory status; each one presents new challenges and opportunities. Price changes, style guide adjustments, and color corrections - it's exhilarating to promote the beautiful cast metal designs. As a lover of Christmas, I get to celebrate twice: designing the holiday catalogs from August-October for Viridian Bay, then I celebrate again in real time 🎄.

Adjusting spreads to reflect the season is a fun challenge. At the end of 2020 with holiday sales around the corner, shipping disruptions prevented us from promoting traditional holiday items. I had the intriguing task of fitting an alligator serving tray into a gift catalog. I am very proud to say that the cookie-nibbling alligator tray was a sell-out that December (picture above).
